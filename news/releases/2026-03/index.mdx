We are truly thrilled to share the news of our first openRuyi release! We appreciate your support and enthusiasm as we embark on this journey together.

The majority of packages in this release are compatible with the `rva23u64` target, featuring LTO enabled and using `-mtune=generic-ooo` as the default tuning option.

{/* truncate */}

## Core Components & Toolchains

* Kernel (mainline): 6.19.9

* Kernel (LTS): 6.18.19

* gcc: 15.2.0 with RVA23 support

* binutils: 2.45

* glibc: 2.42

* grub: 2.14

* systemd: 259

* coreutils: 9.9

* util-linux: 2.41.3

## Other

* Openresty LuaJIT: v2.1-20260114 with riscv64 support

* zlib-ng: 2.3.3 with riscv-hwprobe support

* labwc: 0.9.3

* wayland: 1.24.0

* mesa: 26.0.1

## Images

We offer four editions tailored for different scenarios:

* Server: Designed for use on bare-metal servers.

* Container: For both public and private cloud environments.

* Desktop: Proudly uses labwc as our premier desktop solution.

* Zero (BSP validation): Special Edition for SoC bringup and kernel development, especially in the pre-Silicon stage.

You can download it from [here](https://releases.openruyi.cn/creek/2026.03/rva23/).

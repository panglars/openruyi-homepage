# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date**: March 19, 2026

Welcome to the openRuyi Project. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your information transparently. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and handle the data generated when you visit our website.

## Information We Collect

When you visit the openRuyi Project, our servers automatically record basic connection information (standard server logs). We only collect the following data:

* Your IP address

* Your browser type and version (User Agent)

* The time of your visit and the pages you request

## How We Use This Information

The sole purposes of collecting this data are to:

* Analyze internal website traffic and usage trends.

* Monitor and ensure the security and stability of our website.

We do not use this data to track your personal identity.

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We promise that we never sell, rent, or share your access data with any third-party service providers or individuals. All data is strictly kept internal within the openRuyi Project for the purposes stated above.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please feel free to contact us at: [privacy@openruyi.cn](mailto:privacy@openruyi.cn)
